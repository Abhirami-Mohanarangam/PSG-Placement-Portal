Words from our Dean
Greetings from the Placement Office of PSG College of Technology!

Thank you for your interest in our institution. Our placement office strives to ensure the best environment for companies to conduct their recruitment drives, smoothly and efficiently. We facilitate recruitment drives in both in-campus and virtual modes, to enhance the placement potential of every graduating student of PSG College of Technology. Our placement office is equipped with the state-of-the-art facilities to conduct all placement related activities such as Pre-Placement Talks, Written / Online Tests, Group Discussions and Interviews. Our office guides and counsels students of our institution for successful placement and career.
